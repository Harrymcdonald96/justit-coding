How to Make Tea

  1. Fill the kettle with water.
  2. Boil the water.
  3. Place a teabag in a cup.
  4. Pour boiling water into the cup.
  5. Let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes.
  6. Remove the teabag and enjoy.
